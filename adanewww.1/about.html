About DNA Detective

DNA Detective is a mystery game about uncovering hidden clues through DNA evidence. Explore the crime scene, examine suspects, and connect evidence using DNA alignment algorithms.

How to Play

  1. Scan the scene for clues.
  2. Paste a DNA sample and run the Smith–Waterman algorithm to find matches.
  3. Investigate each suspect's background and evidence trail.
  4. Make your accusation based on findings.
